Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

Step 1/6: Raw Material

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution
Step 2/6: Extrusion

Precision Extrusion

State-of-the-art extruders melt and form the HDPE material with absolute thermal consistency for perfect dimensional control.

Step 3/6: Cooling

Controlled Cooling

Precise water cooling tanks maintain optimal temperature gradients, ensuring uniform crystallization and stress-free pipe walls.

Step 4/6: Sizing

Precision Sizing

Advanced laser gauging and calibration equipment ensure every pipe meets exact dimensional tolerances per ISO standards.

Step 5/6: Quality Control

Rigorous Quality Control

Every batch undergoes hydrostatic pressure testing, dimensional inspection, and material property verification before dispatch.

Step 6/6: Marking

Permanent Marking

Laser-printed markings include size, pressure rating, material grade, and production batch for full traceability.
